About Me

Hardware meets Software

I'm Pritam — a 3rd year Electronics and Communication Engineering student at Silicon University, Bhubaneswar, with a deep fascination for VLSI technology and the semiconductor industry. My academic journey has led me to work hands-on with analog IC design, CMOS characterization, and EDA tools.

Beyond hardware, I enjoy building on the software side too — I've done frontend development with React.js and I'm currently expanding into DevOps, exploring how modern engineering workflows come together.

What drives me is the intersection of low-level circuit design and high-level software systems — building solutions that are both electrically sound and technically elegant.
